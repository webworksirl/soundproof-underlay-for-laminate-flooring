About Us

The increasing prevalence of vibrations generated by machinery has emerged as a significant concern in modern work environments and daily life. Isolgomma's innovative offering, Megamat, addresses these challenges comprehensively, achieving the following objectives:

  1. Reducing Acoustic Pollution: Megamat effectively mitigates the adverse effects of excessive vibrations on the acoustic environment, thereby contributing to a reduction in acoustic pollution.

  2. Enhancing Worker Safety: By minimizing the risks faced by workers consistently exposed to vibrations, Megamat prioritizes the safety and well-being of employees.

  3. Improving Machinery Longevity: Megamat's unique design enhances the overall durability and reliability of machinery, leading to an extended operational lifespan.

These versatile products find their ideal application in demanding scenarios like presses, mallets, engines, compression groups, and air-conditioning units. Serving as highly efficient vibration insulation and damping systems, they provide definitive anti-vibration solutions for precision machinery and instruments, guaranteeing optimal performance.
